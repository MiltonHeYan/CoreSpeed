Information Collection

When you register an account for this application, use the services, or contact us, we may collect information including but not limited to: your basic personal information (such as name, email address), device information, usage data, and location information. We handle your personal information strictly in accordance with laws and regulations.

The information we collect will be used to improve service quality, provide you with personalized content recommendations, and for market and data analysis to help us optimize user experience. If we need to use the information for purposes beyond those described in this policy, we will obtain your consent before using it.

Information Sharing

We will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, unless it is necessary to complete services (such as payment processing) or required by laws and regulations. We take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. You should understand and accept this risk when using this application.

We will only retain your personal information for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. After your account is deleted or services are terminated, we may continue to retain some information as permitted by law for audit, legal, or security purposes.

User Rights

Under applicable laws, you may have rights to access, modify, delete, and restrict the processing of your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights or have any questions about the privacy policy, please contact us using the contact information in this policy. We will respond to your requests in a timely manner according to relevant laws and regulations.

Children's Privacy

The services of this application are not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with information without your consent, please contact us promptly, and we will delete the relevant information as soon as possible.

This privacy policy may be updated periodically due to service adjustments or legal requirements. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the application or via email. Continuing to use this application indicates your agreement to the latest terms of the privacy policy.
